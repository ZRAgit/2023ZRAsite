Zeichner Risk Analytics (ZRA) is located in the tallest building of the three Ballston Plaza office buildings in the northwest quadrant of North Glebe Road and North Fairfax Drive. It is located next to I-66, and is recognizable by a metal statue in the front of the building. Underground parking is available in our building.

  • 4601 Fairfax Drive, Suite 1130
  • Arlington, VA 22203
  • Email: contact@zra.com
  • MON - FRI: 7am - 11pm

    By Car

    As you approach 4601 Fairfax Drive, you will turn onto a side road called North Wakefield Street. North Wakefield St takes you to the front circle in front of 4601 Fairfax Drive. If you are facing the building from the front circle, the parking garage can be found on the right of the building.

    By Metro

    The ABC is located near the Orange Line Ballston Metro stop. If you are arriving at the Ballston Metro stop, face Fairfax Drive, walk down to the intersection of Glebe Road and Fairfax Drive, cross the street, pass Marymount College and turn right on N. Wakefield Street. The building is located after the 3-way stop sign on the left.

    By MetroBus or ART bus

    MetroBus routes 2A,B,C,G; 23A,C; 38B; and ART route 51 make stops on both sides of Glebe Road at North 11th Street near the traffic light. As you face west, proceed straight on North 11th Street and turn left to our building at 4601 Fairfax Drive.
