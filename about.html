ABOUT ZRA

Home > About

ZRA is a trusted partner for Federal government agencies and organizations seeking to safeguard their critical assets. With over 25 years of experience, we deliver exceptional mission support services and innovative cybersecurity solutions. Our interdisciplinary team of cybersecurity experts leverages their diverse backgrounds to address risk assessment and mitigation, capability-based planning, IT transformation, compliance implementation, and education and coaching. We go beyond traditional approaches, offering proactive cybersecurity measures that keep our clients one step ahead of emerging threats. By aligning cybersecurity initiatives with strategic objectives and emphasizing a culture of security awareness, we empower organizations to navigate the complexities of cybersecurity effectively. At ZRA, we foster strong partnerships based on trust, transparency, and shared goals. Our client-centric approach involves understanding unique challenges and providing tailored solutions. We cover the entire cybersecurity lifecycle, from initial assessment to ongoing monitoring and improvement, while staying ahead of industry trends and best practices. Our team's expertise spans law and policy, computer science, business, audit, finance, and engineering. Serving as trusted advisors, we assist government agencies, critical infrastructure companies, Fortune 200 companies, venture capital firms, and more. Join our community of partners and customers dedicated to achieving cybersecurity excellence.

PAST PERFORMANCE

Customer: United States Department of Energy (DoE) Sandia National Laboratories (SNL)

ZRA tracks changes to policies, directives, and leadership to provide relevant updates to NTESS. By leveraging environment awareness, ZRA supports NTESS’ Program Leadership Team in its oversight programs and makes recommendations for strategic planning, technical and programmatic vision, and strategic engagement efforts.

Customer: United States Department of Energy (DoE) Sandia National Laboratories (SNL)

ZRA draws upon unique industry insights and government perspectives on cyber security, risk management, and provides guidance and support on SNL’s projects with DHS CS&C Cyber Programs. This support includes technical writing; quality control; legislative and policy analysis; risk management support; and process definition for supply chain risk management.

Customer: Deutsche Bank (DB)

ZRA assisted in the development of DB’s strategy for overseeing and managing the most complex IT risks. Like its counterparts at other global financial institutions, the DB CISO is reviewing its existing capability models and considering enhancements to address not only changes in the risk environment, but also strategic business and management challenges. Specific factors requiring capability development include: cybersecurity risks that affect corporate performance, pressures to deliver financial and operating efficiencies across stove-piped IT and engineering, and stakeholder management.
