About Dustin

Mr. Ward-Dahl joined ZRA in May 2016 and currently serves as a Senior Associate. In his current role, he provides program management and strategic communication support to clients on issues at the intersection of technology and public policy. His subject matter expertise includes analytics development, cloud security, data strategy, and digital transformation.

Before joining ZRA, Mr. Ward-Dahl honed his research and writing skills as a congressional intern for Congresswoman Suzan DelBene and Congressman Tim Walz, focusing on cybersecurity and transportation policy.

Mr. Ward-Dahl received his B.A. in 2014 from Christopher Newport University with a major in Political Science and a minor in Business Administration.
