COMPLIANCE IMPLEMENTATION

Capabilities > Compliance Implementation

SUMMARY

ZRA is your trusted partner for comprehensive compliance implementation and support services. We understand the importance of adhering to regulatory requirements, best practices, and standards across government and private sectors. Our seasoned experts work closely with your organization to assess existing processes, identify gaps, and develop customized strategies to achieve and sustain compliance. We assist in designing and implementing controls, policies, and procedures while providing thorough training and ongoing support. With our expertise, you can streamline your compliance efforts, minimize risks, and foster a culture of unwavering compliance throughout your organization.

In addition, we offer policy drafting and support services tailored to the specific needs of government entities and the private sector. Our team collaborates closely with clients to develop policies that align with their goals and compliance standards. Whether it's security, data privacy, or other areas, we customize policies to address your unique challenges and obligations. Our aim is to help you establish effective policies that enhance operational efficiency, mitigate risks, and ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations. Furthermore, we provide services such as Privacy Act/FOIA notices, ZTMM compliance, OMB 21-31 Security Logs compliance, internal controls design and implementation, Yellow Book support, and internal audits and reporting. With our comprehensive support, you can navigate the complexities of compliance with confidence and achieve your organizational objectives while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and security.

ZRA Manages IT

ZRA develops customized solutions to manage your organization’s information technology. Our custom tools provide leadership with visibility into CISA’s IT performance. We design metrics that generate insights into cybersecurity policies, operational posture, and compliance with DHS requirements. We provide technology asset management, privacy and information-sharing compliance management, and network configuration management.

ZRA Can Manage the Full Systems Engineering Lifecycle

ZRA has created adaptable programs to help CISA assess its organizational processes. Our programs go beyond simply implementing standard process improvement activities, focusing instead on complete understanding of CISA’s mission, strategy, and customers. Our approach provides a mix of strategic and short-term recommendations for implementation.

ZRA Manages People

ZRA can also help manage CISA’s unique and dynamic workforce in alignment with DHS policy and requirements, particularly the directives on Cybersecurity Workforce Management Support and Employee Performance Management. To this end, we can help design, validate, and implement workforce evaluation tools to help improve policies.
