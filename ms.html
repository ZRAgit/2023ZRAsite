SUMMARY

ZRA specializes in delivering mission support services customized for the Cyber & Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA). ZRA’s deeply experienced and credentialed practitioners focus on the unique issues that CISA confronts in developing new capabilities, services, and competencies. We maintain fluency in CISA needs through our experience within each of the major CISA executive offices, understanding of CISA programs, and cleared staff that have suitability as well as an appreciation for the complexity of CISA’s national mission. This specialization facilitates effective and cost-efficient mission support.

ZRA is Familiar with CISA Needs

ZRA has significant experience in building and managing complex programs with the Federal Government, and with CISA specifically. We have worked on projects across CISA, supporting CSD, NRMC, and SED. This gives us valuable insight into the agency’s strategic and operational needs. We can anticipate issues and mitigate them in advance, enabling mission resilience. We also have strong awareness of CISA’s operating environment, from stakeholder requirements to emerging threat trends to changes in the policy/regulatory landscape. With our comprehensive focus on CISA- relevant governance, interagency processes, policy, political institutions and engagement strategies, we ensure projects run seamlessly.

ZRA’s credentialed practitioners are fluent in the language and landscape unique to CISA, which allows us to meet the Agency’s needs on a shorter learning curve and with greater effectiveness than generic consulting companies. Our experience integrating planning and execution activities adds accountability and efficiency throughout the project lifecycle to ensure mission objectives, cost, budget, and key stakeholder requirements align throughout the process.
