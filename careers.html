WHAT WE DO

At ZRA, we surpass traditional cybersecurity solutions. We actively drive change, guiding organizations towards a secure and resilient future. Our team excels in conducting thorough risk assessments, identifying vulnerabilities, and developing effective risk mitigation strategies. We believe in taking an initiative-taking approach to cybersecurity, enabling our clients to stay one step ahead of emerging threats. By leveraging industry-leading frameworks and methodologies, we ensure that our clients not only meet compliance requirements but also adopt tailored best practices.

Moreover, our capability-based planning services help organizations chart a clear path towards a robust cybersecurity posture. With a deep understanding of the federal government landscape, we assist our clients in aligning their cybersecurity initiatives with their overarching strategic objectives. Leveraging our interdisciplinary expertise, we consider technical, operational, and policy perspectives to design comprehensive solutions that withstand the test of time. Our team takes pride in cultivating a culture of security awareness within organizations by providing education and coaching to equip personnel with the knowledge and skills to effectively combat cyber threats.

CAREERS

Research Associate

As a Research Associate at ZRA, your role will focus on providing strategic guidance to clients. You will be responsible for conducting comprehensive research and analysis, gaining a deep understanding of their unique cybersecurity challenges, and aligning their initiatives with overarching strategic objectives. By leveraging your expertise and knowledge of the government landscape, you will assist clients in developing effective risk assessment frameworks, identifying vulnerabilities, and crafting tailored risk mitigation strategies.

Research Assistant

Joining ZRA as a Research Assistant, you will contribute to our mission of assisting clients in aligning their cybersecurity initiatives with their strategic objectives in the federal government sector. Your responsibilities will include conducting extensive research, analyzing cybersecurity trends, and assisting in the development of risk assessment methodologies. By providing valuable insights and supporting our expert team, you will help clients gain a comprehensive understanding of their cybersecurity landscape and identify vulnerabilities that may impact their strategic objectives.

Communications

As a Communications professional at ZRA your role will be essential in crafting visually captivating and impactful materials. With a focus on graphic-heavy content, you will utilize your creative mind to design compelling visuals that effectively convey complex cybersecurity concepts. Collaborating with our team, you will develop strategic communication plans, manage various channels, and ensure that our clients and stakeholders are informed about ZRA's ability to align cybersecurity initiatives with strategic objectives. Your expertise in graphic design and visual storytelling will play a crucial role in enhancing our brand image and showcasing our deep understanding of cybersecurity management in the federal government sector.

Internship

ZRA offers valuable internship opportunities for aspiring professionals interested in the strategic aspect of cybersecurity management in the federal government sector. As an intern, you will gain exposure to ZRA's expertise in assisting clients in aligning their cybersecurity initiatives with overarching strategic objectives. Your role will involve conducting research, supporting risk assessment processes, and contributing to the development of risk mitigation strategies tailored to the federal government landscape. This internship will provide you with a unique understanding of the strategic implications of cybersecurity and the opportunity to work alongside experts in the field, positioning you for a successful career in this specialized domain.
