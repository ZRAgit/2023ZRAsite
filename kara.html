About Kara

Kara Brooks serves as the Program Director for Business Operations at Zeichner Risk Analytics, overseeing business implementation and daily operations. Prior to ZRA, Ms. Brooks applied her expertise in human resources and program management across several agencies within the U.S. Intelligence Community. Ms. Brooks focused on strategic human capital, talent acquisition, process improvement and business systems integration in support of the Office of the Director of National Intelligence, the National Geospatial Intelligence Agency, and the Defense Counterintelligence and Security Agency.

Ms. Brooks holds a Bachelor of Science in Management, with concentrations in Business Law and Public Policy; and earned a Master of Science in Corporate and Organizational Development from Northeastern University. Ms. Brooks is a Senior Certified Professional through the Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM-SCP).
