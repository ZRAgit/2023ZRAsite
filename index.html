ABOUT ZRA

ZRA has an experienced team of strategic thinkers with law and policy, computer science, business, audit, finance, and engineering backgrounds. ZRA’s dynamic team provides the government and leading critical infrastructure companies with innovative approaches to address cybersecurity challenges. ZRA is a trusted advisor to senior leaders and executives in both the public and private sectors.

Learn More

ZRA specializes in delivering mission support services customized for the Cyber & Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA). ZRA’s deeply experienced and credentialed practitioners focus on the unique issues that CISA confronts in developing new capabilities, services, and competencies. We maintain fluency in CISA needs through our experience within each of the major CISA executive offices, understanding of CISA programs, and cleared staff that have suitability as well as an appreciation for the complexity of CISA’s national mission. This specialization facilitates effective and cost-efficient mission support.

ZRA provides leading-edge financial and acquisition support to help CISA secure consistent funding and maximize efficiency amidst expanding operational needs and tightening budgets. ZRA fully understands the strategic trends that are impacting CISA’s budget. We can formulate budget plans that meet current and future needs. We also have expertise in managing acquisitions to ensure compliance and efficiency. Our subject matter expertise includes business, law, technology, and policy, informing our finance and acquisition capabilities with a holistic view on business, strategy, and operations.

ZRA provides cybersecurity education and training customized to CISA’s needs. Our proprietary IT/Cyber Content was researched and created in house by highly experienced engineers and practitioners. ZRA offers learning experiences in many formats, to adapt to the tight schedule of your organization. You can rely on our efficient process and highly qualified Subject Matter Experts to deliver a learning experience that is engaging and effective.

ZRA leverages broad expertise in systems engineering and deep understanding of CISA’s programs and policies to provide tailored support to systems engineering capability development. Unlike other firms, ZRA has cultivated deep knowledge of CISA’s programs, mission, and systems engineering doctrine. We have developed adaptable management processes and tools to promote integration and synergy across the Enterprise. We help manage complex design projects and architectures from concept design to full operational capability.

ZRA provides quality risk management support services for CISA. ZRA has expertise in assessing threat, vulnerability, and consequence to help CISA manage risks across the enterprise. Our experienced team can help design and implement risk management processes to advance CISA’s mission as the Nation’s risk advisor.

ZRA works with CISA to design, implement, and promote network architectures. ZRA understands the current state and overall trajectory of Federal networks and the intrinsic need for constant realignment based on shifting threats. ZRA has deep understanding of technical requirements and best practices for implementing resilient network architectures. ZRA’s deep technical expertise and project management experience aids CISA in translating conceptual frameworks into workable designs that are implemented with precision and are designed to be easily upgraded as new threats arise.
